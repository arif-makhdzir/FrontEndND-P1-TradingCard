Cats

Cats are dangerous predators yet they are domesticated and able to live with human as a companion

  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: Forest

Brown in color with black stripes. It has a beatiful paur of green eyes with sharp, pointy ears. Its tail is long and black in color. It is very fat and well-fed. It likes to hunt for birds and rats, making it an ideal animal to have.
